About me

I am a self-taught web developer driven by a passion for creating dynamic and engaging user experiences. I wrote my first piece of code in the autumn of 2022 and I have never looked back. Continuously embracing new technologies, I enjoy applying them to solve problems and deliver innovative solutions.

I graduated from Andrei Neagoie's 'The Complete Web Developer: Zero to Mastery' in the spring of 2023. Since then I have actively engaged in personal and collaborative projects to further enhance my skills as a developer, with a strong emphasis on JavaScript.

Prior to my web development journey, I worked as a Technical Author in the Aerospace sector. However, in 2022, I made the decision to retrain and redirect my focus towards web development, a decision that has led me here!
